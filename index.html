Universe — Rulzethar Overview

Rulzethar is a living planet where sentient cities and bio-architectures fuse with its native species. Its energy cores—Aether Wells—enable low-friction travel and extraordinary defense systems. Social structures emphasize collective stewardship over extraction.

Social Order
City-Clusters governed by Councils of Keepers. Knowledge shared via Zethariq dialects and neural glyphs.
Technology
Bioplasma engineering, aetheric shielding and symbiotic exosuits. Weapons are rarely kinetic—often disruptive to invasive control systems.

Earth-13 — The Hidden Council

Earth-13 operates through obscured financial networks, front corporations and covert research programs. To Rulzethar they appear as innocuous trade missions, while in truth they prepare to harvest Aether Wells and biological tech.

Structure
13 directors spanning finance, energy, defense, media and governance. Publicly absent, privately commanding.
Modus Operandi
Covert infiltration, data-corruption, sleeper agents and economic destabilization to force cooperation.
Primary Objective
Acquire Rulzethar Aether Wells to control interstellar influence and unlock regenerative tech.

How they hide

  • Shell corporations with plausible environmental front projects
  • AI-driven disinformation + narrative laundering
  • Black-market tech procurement and clandestine labs
